Options guide:

Clicking on the options button will open this window:

  • Strength: Controls the grey text detection for the website. A higher value may detect more grey text, but may also darken light text against dark backgrounds.
  • Whitelist: Adds the website to the whitelist. Dragging the slider or toggling another option will automatically check this option.
  • Blacklist: Adds the website to the blacklist.
  • Bold text: Changes the font weight of all text to bold.
  • Skip colored text: Attempts to avoid non-grey text. Affected by strength.
  • Skip headings: Avoids all big headings.
  • Advanced dimming: Dims text to a darker shade of its color (rather than just black) based on the Strength value. Can be slow on heavy websites.
  • Force placeholder dimming: Forces all placeholder text to turn black, instead of just increasing their opacity. Useful in websites where placeholders are still grey with the add-on enabled.
  • Form outline: Attempts to add a black outline around text areas.
  • More options: Opens a tab with the default options and the whitelist/blacklist.
