Welcome to Font Contrast!

The app has two buttons:

  • The button in the add-on bar toggles the add-on for the current tab. It turns black to indicate activation.
  • The button in the address bar manages the options for the current page.

Options guide:

Clicking on the options button will open this window:

  • Strength: Adjusts the detection of dark backgrounds. A higher value might catch more hard to read text, but it might also dim light text against dark backgrounds.
  • Increase size (%): Enlarges fonts that are below the size threshold.
  • Whitelist: Adds the website to the whitelist. Dragging a slider or toggling another option will automatically check this option.
  • Blacklist: Adds the website to the blacklist.
  • Avoid colorful text: Attempts to avoid colorful text and links, if they are considered readable.
  • Bold text: Changes the font weight of all text to bold.
  • Skip headings: Avoids dimming all big headings.
  • Advanced mode: Dims text to a darker shade of its color (rather than just black). Can cause slowdowns on big websites.
  • Force max opacity: Forces full opacity on all fonts.
  • Force black placeholder: Forces all placeholder text to turn black, rather than just increasing their opacity.
  • Form border: Attempts to add a black outline around text areas.
  • More options: Opens a tab with all the options, including the ability to activate the add-on everywhere, the global options (with in-depth tooltips) and the whitelist/blacklist.
